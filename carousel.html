Hinduism history

Most scholars believe Hinduism started somewhere between 2300 B.C. and 1500 B.C.
in the Indus Valley, near modern-day Pakistan. But many Hindus argue that their faith
is timeless and has always existed. Unlike other religions, Hinduism
has no one founder but is instead a fusion of various beliefs.
